Together for a Cleaner & Smarter Ethiopia

It's a national system developed for maintaining Ethiopia in a clean and sustainable manner. By interlinking the citizens, communities, and local authorities together. we create a smarter, greener future together.

Clean Today, Green Tomorrow

Our goal is to bring together communities, citizens, and local authorities for a clean, healthy, and sustainable Ethiopia. We use innovative technology and create awareness to minimize waste, recycle, and grow green for the future. Every action counts, and together we can make our cities smarter and our environment safer.
